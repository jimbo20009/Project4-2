Reduced mobility does not have to mean reduced freedom

Airlines offer personal assistance to those who ask for it, but many people prefer to get around on their own.

The ABLE to GO mobile app can help you:

  • find accessible paths through airports
  • move at your own pace and still be on time for flights
  • access essential airport services
  • receive alerts
